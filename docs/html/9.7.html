    • Therefore:
      • endpoints must be checked separately when needed.

  • [2:18:36]. Why $\dfrac{1}{1-рқ’ҷ}$ is useful.

      • $\dfrac{1}{1-рқ’ҷ}$

    • Geometric series formula:
      • $\dfrac{1}{1-рқ’“}=1+рқ’“+рқ’“^{2}+рқ’“^{3}+\cdots$
    • In this case:
      • $рқ’“=рқ’ҷ$
    • Therefore:
      • $\dfrac{1}{1-рқ’ҷ}=1+рқ’ҷ+рқ’ҷ^{2}+рқ’ҷ^{3}+\cdots$
    • Series notation:
      • $\dfrac{1}{1-рқ’ҷ}=\displaystyle \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}рқ’ҷ^{n}$

      • $\dfrac{1}{1-рқ’ҷ}=\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}рқ’ҷ^{n-1}$
  • [2:21:58]. Why the interval is $(-1,1)$.
