в—Ҹ [1:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=60). рҹ§© Example 1 вҖ” Definition of an alternating series. 
     в—ү Given series:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№/рқ’Ҹ
     в—ү Expand the first terms:
          в—Ӣ 1 - 1/2 + 1/3 - 1/4 + 1/5 - ...
     в—ү Key observation:
          в—Ӣ the signs alternate:
               в–  +, -, +, -, +, ...
          в—Ӣ therefore, this is an alternating series.

в—Ҹ Identifying the alternating factor.
     в—ү The factor that creates the sign change is:
          в—Ӣ (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№
     в—ү If the series starts with a positive term,
          в—Ӣ we often use (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№.
     в—ү If the series starts with a negative term,
          в—Ӣ we often use (-1)вҒҝ.
     в—ү Key idea:
          в—Ӣ the (-1) factor controls the signs.
          в—Ӣ the rest of the expression gives the positive size of each term.

в—Ҹ [3:20](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=200). General structure of an alternating series.
     в—ү Any alternating series can usually be written as:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№ вӢ… рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Here:
          в—Ӣ (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№ creates the alternating signs.
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ represents the positive part of the terms.
     в—ү In this example:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 1/рқ’Ҹ
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№/рқ’Ҹ = вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№ вӢ… 1/рқ’Ҹ
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ itself is positive.
          в—Ӣ the alternating sign is not considered part of рқ’ӮвӮҷ.

в—Ҹ [4:17](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=257). Harmonic Series versus Alternating Harmonic Series.
     в—ү Harmonic Series:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] 1/рқ’Ҹ
          в—Ӣ = 1 + 1/2 + 1/3 + 1/4 + ...
          в—Ӣ diverges.
     в—ү Alternating Harmonic Series:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№/рқ’Ҹ
          в—Ӣ = 1 - 1/2 + 1/3 - 1/4 + ...
          в—Ӣ converges.
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ adding alternating signs can change the convergence behavior.
          в—Ӣ the harmonic series diverges,
               в–  but the alternating harmonic series converges.

в—Ҹ [7:53](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=473). The Alternating Series Test.
     в—ү The Alternating Series Test applies to series of the form:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№ вӢ… рқ’ӮвӮҷ
          в—Ӣ or вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝ вӢ… рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Where:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ > 0
     в—ү The test checks two things:
          в—Ӣ the positive terms рқ’ӮвӮҷ must be decreasing.
          в—Ӣ the positive terms рқ’ӮвӮҷ must approach 0.

в—Ҹ [9:24](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=564). Condition 1 вҖ” Decreasing sequence.
     в—ү The positive sequence must be decreasing:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ each next positive term is less than or equal to the previous one.
     в—ү This is important because:
          в—Ӣ the alternating partial sums move back and forth,
          в—Ӣ but the jumps get smaller and smaller.

в—Ҹ [10:30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=630). Condition 2 вҖ” Limit of the positive terms.
     в—ү The positive terms must approach 0:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 0
     в—ү This is like the Divergence Test,
          в—Ӣ but applied to the positive part рқ’ӮвӮҷ.
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ do not include (-1)вҒҝ or (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№ when checking this limit.
          в—Ӣ only use the positive part рқ’ӮвӮҷ.

в—Ҹ [11:26](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=686). Conclusion of the Alternating Series Test.
     в—ү iМІfМІ  рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷ  aМІnМІdМІ  limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 0
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the alternating series вҲ‘ (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№рқ’ӮвӮҷ converges.
     в—ү In words:
          в—Ӣ if the positive terms decrease to 0,
               в–  then the alternating series converges.

в—Ҹ [11:26](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=686). Recap вҖ” How to use the Alternating Series Test.
     в—ү First, verify that the series is alternating.
          в—Ӣ the signs must alternate:
               в–  +, -, +, -, ...
               в–  or -, +, -, +, ...
          в—Ӣ this usually comes from:
               в–  (-1)вҒҝ
               в–  or (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№
     в—ү Then, separate the series into:
          в—Ӣ the alternating factor,
          в—Ӣ and the positive part рқ’ӮвӮҷ.
     в—ү General form:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘ (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№рқ’ӮвӮҷ
          в—Ӣ or вҲ‘ (-1)вҒҝрқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is only the positive part.
          в—Ӣ do not include (-1)вҒҝ or (-1)вҒҝвҒ»В№ inside рқ’ӮвӮҷ.
     в—ү Step 1 вҖ” Check the limit of the positive terms:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 0
     в—ү Step 2 вҖ” Check that the positive terms are decreasing:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ рқ’ӮвӮҷ вҶ’ 0,
          в—Ӣ aМІnМІdМІ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing,
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ the alternating series converges.
     в—ү Key idea:
          в—Ӣ the test is not applied to the whole signed term.
          в—Ӣ it is applied to the positive sequence рқ’ӮвӮҷ.
          
в—Ҹ [13:58](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=838). Graphical and conceptual justification.
     в—ү Imagine the partial sums moving back and forth:
          в—Ӣ add a positive term,
          в—Ӣ subtract a smaller term,
          в—Ӣ add an even smaller term,
          в—Ӣ subtract an even smaller term,
          в—Ӣ and so on.
     в—ү Because the terms are decreasing:
          в—Ӣ each jump is smaller than the previous jump.
     в—ү Because the terms approach 0:
          в—Ӣ the jumps eventually become tiny.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the partial sums settle toward one finite value.
          в—Ӣ this is why the alternating series converges.

в—Ҹ [13:58](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=838). Ping-pong model.
     в—ү Think of a ping-pong rally:
          в—Ӣ the ball moves from one side to the other.
          в—Ӣ each hit travels a shorter distance.
          в—Ӣ eventually, the movement gets closer and closer to a central point.
     в—ү Alternating series behave similarly:
          в—Ӣ the partial sums jump above and below the final sum.
          в—Ӣ the jumps become smaller.
          в—Ӣ the sequence of partial sums converges.

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Even and odd partial sums in an alternating series.
     в—ү Example:
          в—Ӣ 1 - 1/2 + 1/3 - 1/4 + 1/5 - 1/6 + ...
     в—ү Partial sums:
          в—Ӣ SвӮҒ = 1
          в—Ӣ SвӮӮ = 0.5
          в—Ӣ SвӮғ вүҲ 0.833
          в—Ӣ SвӮ„ вүҲ 0.583
          в—Ӣ SвӮ… вүҲ 0.783
          в—Ӣ SвӮҶ вүҲ 0.616
     в—ү Even partial sums:
          в—Ӣ SвӮӮ, SвӮ„, SвӮҶ, ...
          в—Ӣ 0.5, 0.583, 0.616, ...
          в—Ӣ these are increasing.
     в—ү Odd partial sums:
          в—Ӣ SвӮҒ, SвӮғ, SвӮ…, ...
          в—Ӣ 1, 0.833, 0.783, ...
          в—Ӣ these are decreasing.
     в—ү Key pattern:
          в—Ӣ the even partial sums approach the final sum from below.
          в—Ӣ the odd partial sums approach the final sum from above.
          в—Ӣ both subsequences move toward the same value.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the partial sums bounce around the final sum,
               в–  but each bounce is smaller.
          в—Ӣ this is why the alternating series converges.

в—Ҹ [21:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=1260). вқ¶ Check the limit condition.

в—Ҹ [28:50](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=1730). Checking decreasing behavior.
     в—ү To check decreasing, we need:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Direct substitution can be messy:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ = 2(рқ’Ҹ + 1)/(4(рқ’Ҹ + 1) - 1)
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ = (2рқ’Ҹ + 2)/(4рқ’Ҹ + 3)
     в—ү Comparing this directly with:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 2рқ’Ҹ/(4рқ’Ҹ - 1)
     в—ү can be more work.
     в—ү Easier method:
          в—Ӣ model the positive sequence with a function.
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) = 2рқ’ҷ/(4рқ’ҷ - 1)

в—Ҹ [29:20](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=1760). Using the derivative to show decreasing.
     в—ү Differentiate:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) = 2рқ’ҷ/(4рқ’ҷ - 1)
     в—ү Using the quotient rule:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) = [2(4рқ’ҷ - 1) - 4(2рқ’ҷ)]/(4рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ
     в—ү Simplify:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) = (8рқ’ҷ - 2 - 8рқ’ҷ)/(4рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) = -2/(4рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ (4рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ > 0
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) < 0
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) is decreasing.
          в—Ӣ so рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing.

в—Ҹ [31:42](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=1902). Check the limit condition.
     в—ү Compute:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ 2рқ’Ҹ/(4рқ’Ҹ - 1)
     в—ү Since the numerator and denominator have the same degree:
          в—Ӣ compare leading coefficients.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ 2рқ’Ҹ/(4рқ’Ҹ - 1) = 2/4 = 1/2
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ 1/2 вү  0
     в—ү The positive terms do not approach 0.

в—Ҹ [32:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=1938). Conclusion for Example 2.
     в—ү Even though рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing,
          в—Ӣ the limit condition fails.
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ вү  0
     в—ү The series fails the necessary condition for convergence.
     в—ү Therefore:

          в—Ӣ here, the decreasing condition works,

          в—Ӣ вҲһ/вҲһ
     в—ү Apply L'HГҙpital's Rule:
          в—Ӣ derivative of вҲҡ(рқ’Ҹ + 1) = 1/(2вҲҡ(рқ’Ҹ + 1))
          в—Ӣ derivative of рқ’Ҹ - 1 = 1
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ вҲҡ(рқ’Ҹ + 1)/(рқ’Ҹ - 1)
          в—Ӣ = limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ 1/(2вҲҡ(рқ’Ҹ + 1))
          в—Ӣ = 0
     в—ү So:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 0

в—Ҹ [38:45](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=2325). Check the decreasing condition using the derivative.
     в—ү Define:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) = вҲҡ(рқ’ҷ + 1)/(рқ’ҷ - 1)
     в—ү We use a function because:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) models the positive sequence рқ’ӮвӮҷ.
     в—ү If:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) < 0
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) is decreasing,
          в—Ӣ so the sequence рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing.

в—Ҹ [42:32](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=2552). Showing the derivative is negative.
     в—ү Differentiate:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) = вҲҡ(рқ’ҷ + 1)/(рқ’ҷ - 1)
     в—ү Important domain detail:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) is not defined at рқ’ҷ = 1,
               в–  because the denominator рқ’ҷ - 1 becomes 0.
          в—Ӣ since the series starts at рқ’Ҹ = 2,
               в–  we only need to show decreasing behavior for рқ’ҷ вүҘ 2.
     в—ү Using the quotient rule:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) = [((рқ’ҷ - 1)/(2вҲҡ(рқ’ҷ + 1))) - вҲҡ(рқ’ҷ + 1)]/(рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ
     в—ү Combine the numerator:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) = (рқ’ҷ - 1 - 2(рқ’ҷ + 1))/(2вҲҡ(рқ’ҷ + 1)(рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ)
     в—ү Simplify:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) = (-рқ’ҷ - 3)/(2вҲҡ(рқ’ҷ + 1)(рқ’ҷ - 1)ВІ)
     в—ү Sign analysis:
          в—Ӣ the denominator is positive for рқ’ҷ > 1.
          в—Ӣ the numerator -рқ’ҷ - 3 is negative for рқ’ҷ > 1.
          в—Ӣ therefore, рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) < 0 for рқ’ҷ > 1.
     в—ү In particular:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҮвҖІ(рқ’ҷ) < 0 for рқ’ҷ вүҘ 2.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) is decreasing on [2,вҲһ).
          в—Ӣ so рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing for рқ’Ҹ вүҘ 2.

в—Ҹ [44:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=2658). Conclusion for Example 3.
     в—ү The series is alternating.
     в—ү The positive terms satisfy:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 0
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing.
     в—ү Therefore, by the Alternating Series Test:
          в—Ӣ the series converges.
     в—ү Note:
          в—Ӣ even if the first few terms are problematic,
               в–  adding or removing finitely many terms does not change convergence.

в—Ҹ [45:28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=2728). Finding error in alternating series.
     в—ү If a series вҲ‘ рқ’ӮвӮҷ converges, 
          в—Ӣ then it has some sum рқ‘ә.
     в—ү The sequence of partial sums satisfies:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ‘әвӮҷ = рқ‘ә
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ (рқ‘ә - рқ‘әвӮҷ) = 0
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ if рқ’Ҹ actually goes to infinity,
               в–  the partial sums approach the exact sum.
          в—Ӣ but if we stop at a finite рқ’Ҹ,
               в–  there will be some error between рқ‘ә and рқ‘әвӮҷ.

в—Ҹ [50:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=3000). Defining the error.
     в—ү The error is called:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘№вӮҷ
     в—ү Definition:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘№вӮҷ = рқ‘ә - рқ‘әвӮҷ
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ error = whole sum - partial sum
     в—ү In words:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘ә is the exact infinite sum.
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҷ is the partial sum after рқ’Ҹ terms.
          в—Ӣ рқ‘№вӮҷ is the difference between them.

в—Ҹ [53:36](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=3216). Alternating Series Estimation Theorem.
     в—ү For alternating series only:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| = |рқ‘ә - рқ‘әвӮҷ| вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ the absolute error is less than or equal to the first omitted term.
     в—ү If we stop at рқ‘әвӮҷ,
          в—Ӣ the next positive term is рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the error is no larger than рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ.

в—Ҹ [55:04](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=3304). Why the first omitted term controls the error.
     в—ү Suppose the exact sum is:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘ә = рқ’ӮвӮҒ - рқ’ӮвӮӮ + рқ’ӮвӮғ - ... - рқ’ӮвӮҷ + рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ - рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮӮ + ...
     в—ү The partial sum is:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҷ = рқ’ӮвӮҒ - рқ’ӮвӮӮ + рқ’ӮвӮғ - ... - рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү Therefore, the remaining tail is:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘ә - рқ‘әвӮҷ = рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ - рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮӮ + рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮғ - ...
     в—ү Since the positive terms are decreasing:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮӮ вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮғ вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮӮ
          в—Ӣ and so on.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ The remaining terms alternate signs and partially cancel each other, 
          в—Ӣ and since their sizes decrease, the total remaining error cannot exceed the first omitted term.
     в—ү So:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘ә - рқ‘әвӮҷ| вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Key idea.
     в—ү In an alternating series,
          в—Ӣ the terms after рқ‘әвӮҷ keep alternating signs.
     в—ү Because the positive terms decrease,
          в—Ӣ each new term partially cancels the previous one.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the total error after stopping at рқ‘әвӮҷ
               в–  is controlled by the first term you did not include.

в—Ҹ [1:01:24](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=3684). рҹ§© Example 4 вҖ” Practical problem of precision and error: вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=0,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝ/рқ’Ҹ!
     в—ү Goal:
          в—Ӣ approximate the sum of the series using a partial sum.
     в—ү Desired accuracy:
          в—Ӣ make the error less than 0.0005.
     в—ү Key idea:
          в—Ӣ we first prove that the series converges.
          в—Ӣ then we use the error bound to decide how many terms are needed.

в—Ҹ [1:02:42](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=3762). Check convergence first.
     в—ү The series is alternating because of:
          в—Ӣ (-1)вҒҝ
     в—ү Identify the positive part:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 1/рқ’Ҹ!
     в—ү Check the limit:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ 1/рқ’Ҹ! = 0
     в—ү Check decreasing:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ = 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)!
          в—Ӣ 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)! вүӨ 1/рқ’Ҹ!
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is decreasing.
     в—ү By the Alternating Series Test:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=0,вҲһ] (-1)вҒҝ/рқ’Ҹ! converges.

в—Ҹ [1:06:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=3960). How error works.
     в—ү For a convergent alternating series:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| = |рқ‘ә - рқ‘әвӮҷ| вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ
     в—ү In this example:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷвӮҠвӮҒ = 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)!
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| вүӨ 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)!
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ the error after stopping at рқ‘әвӮҷ
               в–  is no bigger than the first omitted term.
     в—ү Practical interpretation:
          в—Ӣ you tell the series how accurate you want to be.
          в—Ӣ then use рқ‘№вӮҷ to determine how many terms are needed to be that accurate.

в—Ҹ [1:09:50](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=4190). Finding how many terms are needed.
     в—ү We want:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| < 0.0005
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| вүӨ 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)!
     в—ү It is enough to require:
          в—Ӣ 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)! < 0.0005
     в—ү This guarantees:
          в—Ӣ |рқ‘№вӮҷ| < 0.0005

в—Ҹ [1:13:01](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=4381). Solving the inequality.
     в—ү Start with:
          в—Ӣ 1/(рқ’Ҹ + 1)! < 0.0005
     в—ү Take reciprocals:
          в—Ӣ (рқ’Ҹ + 1)! > 1/0.0005
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ 1/0.0005 = 2000
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ (рқ’Ҹ + 1)! > 2000

в—Ҹ [1:15:11](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=4511). Determining рқ’Ҹ.
     в—ү Check factorials:
          в—Ӣ 6! = 720
          в—Ӣ 7! = 5040
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ 6! < 2000
          в—Ӣ 7! > 2000
     в—ү We need:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ҹ + 1 = 7
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ рқ’Ҹ = 6
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ stopping at рқ‘әвӮҶ guarantees an error less than 0.0005.

в—Ҹ [1:15:56](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=4556). Computing the partial sum рқ‘әвӮҶ.
     в—ү Since the series starts at рқ’Ҹ = 0,
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҶ includes the terms from рқ’Ҹ = 0 through рқ’Ҹ = 6.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҶ = 1 - 1/1! + 1/2! - 1/3! + 1/4! - 1/5! + 1/6!
     в—ү Rewrite:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҶ = 1 - 1 + 1/2 - 1/6 + 1/24 - 1/120 + 1/720

в—Ҹ [1:18:20](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhYPrQHDrjk&t=4700). Final approximation.
     в—ү Compute:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҶ =  0.368
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘ә вүҲ 0.368
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҶ is not exactly equal to рқ‘ә.
          в—Ӣ рқ‘әвӮҶ is only a partial sum approximation.
     в—ү But:
          в—Ӣ it is a very good approximation.
     в—ү How good?
          в—Ӣ the error is less than 0.0005.
          в—Ӣ this means рқ‘әвӮҶ is within 0.0005 of the true sum рқ‘ә.
     в—ү In Leonard's words:
          в—Ӣ it is not equal to рқ‘ә,
               в–  but it is within 5 ten-thousandths of рқ‘ә.
          в—Ӣ that is how the error estimate is used.
