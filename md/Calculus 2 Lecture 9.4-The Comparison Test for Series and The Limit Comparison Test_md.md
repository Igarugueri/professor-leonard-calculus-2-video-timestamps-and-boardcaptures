          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ вүӨ рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ every term of рқ’ӮвӮҷ is less than or equal to the corresponding term of рқ’ғвӮҷ.
     в—ү There are two useful scenarios:
          в—Ӣ proving convergence.

в—Ҹ [3:21](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=201). Scenario 1 вҖ” Proving convergence. [рҹ“·image](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.4/[3-21]-01.png)
     в—ү Suppose:
          в—Ӣ 0 вүӨ рқ’ӮвӮҷ вүӨ рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү  iМІfМІ  вҲ‘ рқ’ғвӮҷ converges,
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ вҲ‘ рқ’ӮвӮҷ also converges.
     в—ү Reason:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is smaller than рқ’ғвӮҷ term by term.
          в—Ӣ  iМІfМІ  the larger positive series has a finite sum,
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ the smaller positive series must also have a finite sum.

          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is larger than рқ’ғвӮҷ term by term.

     в—ү Reason:

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Direct Comparison Test summary.
     в—ү To prove convergence:

          в—Ӣ bigger than convergent вҮ’ inconclusive.

в—Ҹ [41:02](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=2462). The Limit Comparison Test.
     в—ү iМІfМІ Direct Comparison is inconclusive,
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ try the Limit Comparison Test.
     в—ү Inconclusive cases:
          в—Ӣ smaller than divergent вҮ’ inconclusive.
          в—Ӣ bigger than convergent вҮ’ inconclusive.
     в—ү Main idea:
          в—Ӣ we compare our given series to a series whose convergence or divergence we already know,
          в—Ӣ and then we take a limit.
     в—ү This test is especially useful when two series have similar end behavior.

в—Ҹ Definition of the Limit Comparison Test.
     в—ү Suppose:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘ рқ’ӮвӮҷ and вҲ‘ рқ’ғвӮҷ have positive terms.
     в—ү Compute:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү iМІfМІ the limit exists aМІnМІdМІ is a finite positive number: 0 < рқ‘і < вҲһ
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ  вҲ‘ рқ’ӮвӮҷ aМІnМІdМІ вҲ‘ рқ’ғвӮҷ have the same convergence behavior.
     в—ү Meaning:
          в—Ӣ they either both converge,
          в—Ӣ or they both diverge.

в—Ҹ [43:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=2620). Meaning of the limit in the LCT.
     в—ү The ratio:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү measures how close the two series are term by term.
     в—ү If the ratio approaches a finite positive number,
          в—Ӣ the terms behave similarly as рқ’Ҹ вҶ’ вҲһ.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ one series predicts the behavior of the other.

в—Ҹ [43:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=2620). Formal proof idea.
     в—ү iМІfМІ  limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ = рқ‘і
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ, by the definition of a limit:
               в–  |рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ - рқ‘і| < Оөрқ‘і
     в—ү Rewrite the absolute value inequality:
          в—Ӣ -Оөрқ‘і < рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ - рқ‘і < Оөрқ‘і
     в—ү Add рқ‘і to every part:
          в—Ӣ рқ‘і - Оөрқ‘і < рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ < рқ‘і + Оөрқ‘і
     в—ү Factor out рқ‘і:
          в—Ӣ (1 - Оө)рқ‘і < рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ < (1 + Оө)рқ‘і
     в—ү Since рқ’ғвӮҷ > 0, multiply every part by рқ’ғвӮҷ:
          в—Ӣ (1 - Оө)рқ‘і вӢ… рқ’ғвӮҷ < рқ’ӮвӮҷ < (1 + Оө)рқ‘і вӢ… рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү Key interpretation:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ is trapped between two positive constant multiples of рқ’ғвӮҷ.
          в—Ӣ therefore, рқ’ӮвӮҷ and рқ’ғвӮҷ must have the same convergence behavior.

в—Ҹ [47:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=2820). How the inequality proves convergence.
     в—ү From the right side of the inequality:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ < (1 + Оө)рқ‘і вӢ… рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү iМІfМІ  вҲ‘ рқ’ғвӮҷ converges,
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ  вҲ‘ (1 + Оө)рқ‘і вӢ… рқ’ғвӮҷ also converges,
               в–  because multiplying by a positive constant does not change convergence.
     в—ү Since рқ’ӮвӮҷ is smaller than a convergent positive series,
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘ рқ’ӮвӮҷ also converges.

в—Ҹ [47:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=2820). How the inequality proves divergence.
     в—ү From the left side of the inequality:
          в—Ӣ (1 - Оө)рқ‘і вӢ… рқ’ғвӮҷ < рқ’ӮвӮҷ
     в—ү iМІfМІ  вҲ‘ рқ’ғвӮҷ diverges,
          в—Ӣ+ tМІhМІeМІnМІ  вҲ‘ (1 - Оө)рқ‘і вӢ… рқ’ғвӮҷ also diverges,
               в–  because multiplying by a positive constant does not change divergence.
     в—ү Since рқ’ӮвӮҷ is bigger than a divergent positive series,
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘ рқ’ӮвӮҷ also diverges.

в—Ҹ Practical use of the LCT.
     в—ү рқ’ӮвӮҷ is the term of the given series.
     в—ү рқ’ғвӮҷ is the term of the known comparison series.
     в—ү Usually:
          в—Ӣ choose рқ’ғвӮҷ from the end behavior of рқ’ӮвӮҷ.
     в—ү Then compute:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ
     
в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Do not misread the LCT.
     в—ү If the LCT limit exists,
          в—Ӣ this does not automatically mean the series converges.
     в—ү It means:
          в—Ӣ the given series and the comparison series have the same behavior.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ if the comparison series converges,
               в–  the given series converges.
          в—Ӣ if the comparison series diverges,
               в–  the given series diverges.

в—Ҹ [50:36](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=3036). рҹ§© Example 4 Continue вҖ” Applying the LCT:  вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] 1/(вҲҡрқ’Ҹ + 1) [рҹ“·image-1](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.4/[50-36]-01.png) [рҹ“·image-2](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.4/[50-36]-02.png)

     в—ү [53:37](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=3217). Computing the LCT.

          в—Ӣ Since:

     в—ү [56:15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=3375). Interpretation of the LCT result in Example 4.

          в—Ӣ Therefore:

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Key warning.
     в—ү In the Limit Comparison Test,
          в—Ӣ the fact that the limit exists does not automatically mean convergence.
     в—ү It only means:
          в—Ӣ the given series behaves like the comparison series.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ if the comparison series converges,
               в–  the given series converges.
          в—Ӣ if the comparison series diverges,
               в–  the given series diverges.

в—Ҹ [49:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=2943). Decision map for choosing series tests.
     в—ү Step 1 вҖ” Always start with the Divergence Test.
          в—Ӣ take the limit of the general term:
               в–  limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ вү  0,
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ the series diverges.
               в–  stop immediately.
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = 0,
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ the test is inconclusive.
               в–  continue with another test.
     в—ү Step 2 вҖ” Check the easy known series.
          в—Ӣ Is it geometric?
               в–  iМІfМІ yes, use the Geometric Series Test.
          в—Ӣ Is it a рқ’‘-series?
               в–  iМІfМІ yes, use the рқ’‘-Series Test.
          в—Ӣ Is it harmonic or similar to harmonic?
               в–  iМІfМІ yes, use the known behavior of the harmonic series.
     в—ү Step 3 вҖ” Consider the Integral Test.
          в—Ӣ Ask:
               в–  can the terms be represented by a function рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)?
          в—Ӣ Check whether рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ) is:
               в–  positive,
               в–  continuous,
               в–  decreasing.
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ these conditions hold,
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ the Integral Test may work well.
     в—ү Step 4 вҖ” Try the Direct Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ To prove convergence:
               в–  check whether the given series is smaller than a known convergent series.
               в–  smaller than convergent вҮ’ convergent.
          в—Ӣ To prove divergence:
               в–  check whether the given series is bigger than a known divergent series.
               в–  bigger than divergent вҮ’ divergent.
     в—ү Step 5 вҖ” Recognize when Direct Comparison is inconclusive.
          в—Ӣ smaller than divergent вҮ’ inconclusive.
          в—Ӣ bigger than convergent вҮ’ inconclusive.
          в—Ӣ in these cases,
               в–  Direct Comparison does not give enough information.
     в—ү Step 6 вҖ” Try the Limit Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ Use this when the given series has similar end behavior to a known series.
          в—Ӣ Choose a known comparison series:
               в–  usually geometric,
               в–  or a рқ’‘-series,
               в–  or harmonic-type.
          в—Ӣ Compute:
               в–  limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ this limit exists and is a finite positive number,
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ both series have the same convergence behavior.
               в–  they both converge or they both diverge.

     в—ү Rewrite:

          в—Ӣ = 1/рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ
     в—ү Therefore, compare with:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] 1/рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ
     в—ү This is a convergent рқ’‘-series because:
          в—Ӣ рқ’‘ = 3/2 > 1

в—Ҹ [1:25:48](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=5148). Setting up the LCT for Advanced Example 2.
     в—ү Let:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ӮвӮҷ = (вҲҡрқ’Ҹ + ln(рқ’Ҹ))/(рқ’ҸВІ + 1)
          в—Ӣ рқ’ғвӮҷ = 1/рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ
     в—ү Compute:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ рқ’ӮвӮҷ/рқ’ғвӮҷ
     в—ү That is:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ [(вҲҡрқ’Ҹ + ln(рқ’Ҹ))/(рқ’ҸВІ + 1)] / [1/рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ]

в—Ҹ [1:26:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=5163). Simplifying the LCT expression.
     в—ү Reciprocate and multiply:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ [(вҲҡрқ’Ҹ + ln(рқ’Ҹ))рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ]/(рқ’ҸВІ + 1)
     в—ү Distribute:
          в—Ӣ вҲҡрқ’Ҹ вӢ… рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ = рқ’ҸВІ
          в—Ӣ ln(рқ’Ҹ) вӢ… рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ = рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ ln(рқ’Ҹ)
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ [рқ’ҸВІ + рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ ln(рқ’Ҹ)]/(рқ’ҸВІ + 1)

в—Ҹ [1:27:07](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=5227). Divide by the largest power.
     в—ү The largest power in the denominator is:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҸВІ
     в—ү Divide numerator and denominator by:
          в—Ӣ рқ’ҸВІ
     в—ү We get:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ [1 + ln(рқ’Ҹ)/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ]/[1 + 1/рқ’ҸВІ]

в—Ҹ [1:28:17](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=5297). Handling ln(рқ’Ҹ)/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ.
     в—ү The expression:
          в—Ӣ ln(рқ’Ҹ)/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ
     в—ү gives the indeterminate form:
          в—Ӣ вҲһ/вҲһ
     в—ү Use L'HГҙpital's Rule:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ ln(рқ’Ҹ)/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ
     в—ү Differentiate numerator and denominator:
          в—Ӣ d/dрқ’Ҹ [ln(рқ’Ҹ)] = 1/рқ’Ҹ
          в—Ӣ d/dрқ’Ҹ [вҲҡрқ’Ҹ] = 1/(2вҲҡрқ’Ҹ)
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ (1/рқ’Ҹ)/(1/(2вҲҡрқ’Ҹ))
          в—Ӣ = limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ 2вҲҡрқ’Ҹ/рқ’Ҹ
          в—Ӣ = limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ 2/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ
          в—Ӣ = 0

в—Ҹ [1:31:17](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=5477). Final LCT limit for Advanced Example 2.
     в—ү Now:
          в—Ӣ limвӮҷвҶ’вҲһ [1 + ln(рқ’Ҹ)/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ]/[1 + 1/рқ’ҸВІ]
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ ln(рқ’Ҹ)/вҲҡрқ’Ҹ вҶ’ 0
          в—Ӣ 1/рқ’ҸВІ вҶ’ 0
     в—ү The limit is:
          в—Ӣ [1 + 0]/[1 + 0] = 1
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the LCT limit exists.

в—Ҹ [1:32:01](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ei8WKMAHky0&t=5521). Conclusion for Advanced Example 2.
     в—ү Since the LCT limit exists,
          в—Ӣ the given series has the same behavior as the comparison series.
     в—ү Since:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] 1/рқ’ҸВібҗҹВІ converges,
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ вҲ‘[рқ’Ҹ=1,вҲһ] (вҲҡрқ’Ҹ + ln(рқ’Ҹ))/(рқ’ҸВІ + 1) also converges.

          в—Ӣ bigger than divergent.
     в—ү Use Limit Comparison when:

          в—Ӣ the series has clear end behavior.
