     в—ү Previous tests often required positive terms:

     в—ү One case is already known:
          в—Ӣ if the signs alternate regularly,
               в–  use the Alternating Series Test.
     в—ү But:

     в—ү If the original series has negative terms,
          в—Ӣ take the absolute value of the terms.
     в—ү This turns all terms into positive terms.
     в—ү Then we can try tests that require positive terms:
          в—Ӣ Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ Limit Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ Integral Test.
          в—Ӣ Ratio Test.
          в—Ӣ Root Test.

в—Ҹ [1:31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=91). **Formal definition of absolute convergence.** [рҹ“·image](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.6/[1-31]-01.png)
     в—ү Given a series:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Take the absolute value of every term:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum |рқ’Ӯ_{n}|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү iМІfМІ  $\displaystyle \sum |рқ’Ӯ_{n}|$ converges,
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ is absolutely convergent.


в—Ҹ [2:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=123). Effect of absolute value.
     в—ү The absolute value changes negative terms into positive terms.
     в—ү Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $|рқ’Ӯ_{1}|+|рқ’Ӯ_{2}|+|рқ’Ӯ_{3}|+\cdots$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү There are no negative terms left.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ absolute convergence studies the convergence of the positive-size version of the series.

в—Ҹ [4:25](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=265). рҹ§© Example вҖ”  Use for things like this: $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{\sin(2рқ‘Ҙ)}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$
$\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү NOT (Alternating,noneincreasin and positive)

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Meaning of absolute convergence.
     в—ү Absolute convergence means:
          в—Ӣ the series still converges even after removing all sign cancellations.
     в—ү This is stronger than ordinary convergence.
     в—ү Reason:
          в—Ӣ if the positive-size version converges,
               в–  then the original signed series definitely converges.

в—Ҹ [рҹ“·image](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.6/[4-25]-01.png)

     в—ү This is an alternating series.
     в—ү It converges by the Alternating Series Test.

в—Ҹ [5:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=303). Take the absolute value.
     в—ү Consider:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}\right|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$

          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}\right|=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}\right|=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$


в—Ҹ [5:35](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=335). Use the рқ’‘-Series Test.
     в—ү The series:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү is a рқ’‘-series with:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’‘=2$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $2>1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$ converges.


в—Ҹ [6:01](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=361). Conclusion.
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}\right|$ converges,
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$ is absolutely convergent.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ we do not only say вҖңconvergent.вҖқ
          в—Ӣ we say вҖңabsolutely convergent,вҖқ because the absolute value series converges.

в—Ҹ [7:19](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=439). рҹ§© Example вҖ” Conditional convergence: $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}$
     в—ү This is the alternating harmonic series.
     в—ү We already know:
          в—Ӣ it converges by the Alternating Series Test.

в—Ҹ [8:24](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=504). Check ordinary convergence.
     в—ү The series is alternating.
     в—ү Positive part:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Check the limit:

     в—ү Check decreasing:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\le \dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore, by the Alternating Series Test:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}$ converges.

     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ this proves ordinary convergence,

в—Ҹ [9:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=550). Check absolute convergence.
     в—ү Take the absolute value:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}\right|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $|(-1)^{n-1}|=1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}\right|=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}$


в—Ҹ [10:13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=613). Harmonic Series.
     в—ү The series:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү is the harmonic series.
     в—ү The harmonic series diverges.
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}\right|$ diverges.


в—Ҹ [10:48](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=648). Important conclusion.
     в—ү The original alternating harmonic series converges:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}$ converges.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү But its absolute value series diverges:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}\right|$ diverges.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the original series is not absolutely convergent.

в—Ҹ [12:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=730). **Definition of conditional convergence.**
     в—ү iМІfМІ the original series $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ converges, aМІnМІdМІ the absolute value series $\displaystyle \sum |рқ’Ӯ_{n}|$ diverges,
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the series $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ is conditionally convergent.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Symbolically:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ converges
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ but $\displaystyle \sum |рқ’Ӯ_{n}|$ diverges
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ is conditionally convergent.


в—Ҹ [13:49](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=829). Conditional convergence for the alternating harmonic series.
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}$ converges,
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү But:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left|\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}\right|=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}$ diverges,
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{рқ’Ҹ}$ is conditionally convergent.


в—Ҹ [15:15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=915). Absolute convergence is stronger than convergence.
     в—ү Absolute convergence is stronger than ordinary convergence.
     в—ү iМІfМІ a series is absolutely convergent,
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ it is definitely convergent.
     в—ү However:
          в—Ӣ a series can converge without being absolutely convergent.
     в—ү That case is called:
          в—Ӣ conditional convergence.

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Three possible outcomes.
     в—ү Divergent:
          в—Ӣ the series does not converge.
     в—ү Conditionally convergent:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ converges,
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ but $\displaystyle \sum |рқ’Ӯ_{n}|$ diverges.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Absolutely convergent:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum |рқ’Ӯ_{n}|$ converges,
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ so $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ also converges.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Conceptual hierarchy:
          в—Ӣ absolute convergence is the strongest form of convergence.
          в—Ӣ conditional convergence is still convergence,
               в–  but it depends on sign cancellation.
          в—Ӣ divergence means there is no convergence.

          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:

     в—ү The Ratio Test compares:
          в—Ӣ the next term $рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}$
          в—Ӣ with the current term $рқ’Ӯ_{n}$.
     в—ү Main expression:

     в—ү Compute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left|\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}}{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}\right|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}$ goes on top.
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ goes on the bottom.
     в—ү Be careful:
          в—Ӣ do not invert the ratio.

в—Ҹ [28:15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=1695). Ratio Test  $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left|\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}}{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}\right|=рқ‘і$ outcomes.
     в—ү iМІfМІ  $рқ‘і<1$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the series is absolutely convergent.
     в—ү  iМІfМІ  $рқ‘і>1$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the series diverges.
     в—ү  iМІfМІ  $рқ‘і=1$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the test is inconclusive.

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Why the Ratio Test is powerful.
     в—ү The Ratio Test includes absolute values.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ it can prove absolute convergence.
     в—ү It is especially useful for:
          в—Ӣ factorials.
          в—Ӣ powers involving $рқ’Ҹ$.
          в—Ӣ expressions where $\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}}{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}$ simplifies nicely.

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Ratio Test versus Divergence Test.
     в—ү Usually, we first check the Divergence Test.
     в—ү But with factorials or complicated powers,
          в—Ӣ the Divergence Test may be awkward.
     в—ү The Ratio Test can often detect:
          в—Ӣ absolute convergence,
          в—Ӣ or divergence,
          в—Ӣ without needing a separate Divergence Test first.
     в—ү If the Ratio Test gives $рқ‘і=1$,
          в—Ӣ then we must try another test.

в—Ҹ [33:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=1980). Set up the Ratio Test.

     в—ү Reciprocate and multiply:

3.2 - пјІпҪҒпҪ”пҪүпҪҸгҖҖпјҙпҪ…пҪ“пҪ”гҖҖпјҘпҪҳпҪҒпҪҚпҪҗпҪҢпҪ…гҖҖпј’

в—Ҹ [рҹ“·image-1](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.6/[43-10]-01.png) [рҹ“·image-2](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.6/[43-10]-02.png)

в—Ҹ [43:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=2590). рҹ§© Example 2 вҖ” Ratio test: $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ!}{рқ’Ҹ^{n}}$.
     в—ү This is not:
          в—Ӣ geometric,
          в—Ӣ a рқ’‘-series,
          в—Ӣ telescoping.
     в—ү The factorial suggests:
          в—Ӣ use the Ratio Test.

в—Ҹ [44:41](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=2681). Set up the Ratio Test.
     в—ү Let:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}=\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ!}{рқ’Ҹ^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}=\dfrac{(рқ’Ҹ+1)!}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n+1}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Compute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left|\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}}{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}\right|$


в—Ҹ [45:46](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=2746). Build the ratio.
     в—ү Substitute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{\dfrac{(рқ’Ҹ+1)!}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n+1}}}{\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ!}{рқ’Ҹ^{n}}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Reciprocate and multiply:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{(рқ’Ҹ+1)!}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n+1}}\cdot \dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{n}}{рқ’Ҹ!}$


в—Ҹ [48:31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=2911). Factorial simplification.
     в—ү Fundamental property:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $(рқ’Ҹ+1)!=(рқ’Ҹ+1)\cdot рқ’Ҹ!$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Substitute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{(рқ’Ҹ+1)\cdot рқ’Ҹ!\cdot рқ’Ҹ^{n}}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n+1}\cdot рқ’Ҹ!}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Cancel:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ!$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{(рқ’Ҹ+1)рқ’Ҹ^{n}}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n+1}}$


в—Ҹ [50:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=3040). Exponent manipulation.
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n+1}=(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n}\cdot (рқ’Ҹ+1)$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Cancel:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ+1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү We get:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{n}}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Rewrite:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}$


в—Ҹ [50:26](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=3026). Special exponential limit.
     в—ү We reached:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Direct substitution gives:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}\to 1^{\infty}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү This is an indeterminate form:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $1^{\infty}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Rewrite the fraction:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}=\dfrac{1}{\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{рқ’Ҹ}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}=\left[\dfrac{1}{\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{рқ’Ҹ}}\right]^{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\dfrac{1}{\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{рқ’Ҹ}=1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}=\dfrac{1}{\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}}$


в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ: Evaluating the classic exponential limit.
     в—ү We know the classic limit:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}=рқ’Ҷ$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{1}{\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҷ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү So:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҷ}$


в—Ҹ [52:30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=3150). Optional derivation of the classic limit.
     в—ү To justify:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}=рқ’Ҷ$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Rewrite using exponentials:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=рқ’Ҷ^{\lim_{n\to\infty}\ln\left(\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}\right)}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Bring the exponent down:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=рқ’Ҷ^{\lim_{n\to\infty} n\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Rewrite as a quotient:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $n\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)=\dfrac{\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)}{\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү This gives the indeterminate form:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $0/0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Apply L'HГҙpital's Rule:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)}{\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}}\cdot\left(\dfrac{-1}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}\right)}{-\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{n}=рқ’Ҷ^{1}=рқ’Ҷ$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Hence:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҷ}$


в—Ҹ [55:13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=3313). Conclusion for Example 2.
     в—ү The Ratio Test limit is:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҷ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҷ}<1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү By the Ratio Test:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ!}{рқ’Ҹ^{n}}$ converges absolutely.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since the terms are already positive:
          в—Ӣ absolute convergence is just convergence here,
          в—Ӣ but the Ratio Test still proves convergence.

          $\rule{0pt}{}$

в—Ҹ [1:05:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=3903). Conclusion for Example 3.

          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\sqrt[n]{\left(\dfrac{2}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\right)^{n}}=\dfrac{2}{рқ’Ҹ+1}$


в—Ҹ [1:17:24](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=4644). Evaluate the limit.
     в—ү Now:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}(2^{3})^{1/n}\cdot \dfrac{2}{рқ’Ҹ+1}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $(2^{3})^{1/n}\to 1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{2}{рқ’Ҹ+1}\to 0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=1\cdot 0=0$


в—Ҹ [1:17:48](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=4668). Conclusion for the Root Test example.
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү And:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $0<1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү By the Root Test:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^{n}2^{n+3}}{(рқ’Ҹ+1)^{n}}$ converges absolutely.

в—Ҹ [1:20:17](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=4817). Summary of all series tests.
     в—ү Main purpose:
          в—Ӣ decide which test to try first.
     в—ү Strategy:
          в—Ӣ do not start with the hardest test.
          в—Ӣ look for the easiest recognizable structure.

в—Ҹ [1:20:55](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=4855). вқ¶ вҖ” Divergence Test.
     в—ү Always check, when practical:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү iМІfМІ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ӯ_{n}\ne 0$
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ diverges.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү iМІfМІ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ӯ_{n}=0$
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the test is inconclusive.
          в—Ӣ continue with another test.

в—Ҹ [1:21:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=4900). вқ· вҖ” By type.
     в—ү Geometric Series.
          в—Ӣ Geometric form:
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯрқ’“^{n}$
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ $|рқ’“|<1$
               в–  It converges
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ $|рқ’“|\ge 1$
               в–  It diverges 
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ it converges, 
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ the sum is:
                    $\rule{0pt}{}$
                    в–Ј $\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ}{1-рқ’“}$
                    $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ Important:
               в–  check convergence before using the sum formula.
     в—ү Telescoping Series.
          в—Ӣ Telescoping series often require:
               в–  partial fractions.
          в—Ӣ Main idea:
               в–  rewrite the terms so that many terms cancel.
          в—Ӣ Then:
               в–  find a formula for the partial sum $рқ‘ә_{n}$.
          в—Ӣ Finally:
               в–  compute $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ‘ә_{n}$.
     в—ү рқ’‘-Series.
          в—Ӣ рқ’‘-series form:
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{рқ’‘}}$
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ $рқ’‘>1$
               в–  It converges
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ $рқ’‘\le 1$
               в–  It diverges
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ $рқ’‘=1$ 
               в–  gives the harmonic series,
                    в–Ј which diverges.

в—Ҹ [1:27:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=5230). вқё вҖ” Integral Test.
     в—ү Use the Integral Test when the terms can be modeled by a function:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}=рқ’Ү(рқ’Ҹ)$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү The function must be:
          в—Ӣ positive,
          в—Ӣ continuous,
          в—Ӣ decreasing.
     в—ү This only needs to hold from some point onward:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $[рқ‘ө,\infty)$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then evaluate:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \int_{рқ‘ө}^{\infty}рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)\cdot рқ’…рқ‘Ҙ$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ Meaning of $рқ‘ө$:
               в–  $рқ‘ө$ is the point from which the final tail of the series is tested.
               в–  the first finitely many terms do not affect convergence or divergence.
               в–  only the infinite tail determines the final behavior.
     в—ү iМІfМІ the integral converges:
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the series converges.
     в—ү iМІfМІ the integral diverges:
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the series diverges.

в—Ҹ [1:29:55](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=5395). вқ№ вҖ” Comparison Tests.
     в—ү Use Comparison Tests when:
          в—Ӣ all terms are positive,
          в—Ӣ and the series behaves like a known series.
     в—ү Common known series:
          в—Ӣ рқ’‘-series.
          в—Ӣ geometric series.
          в—Ӣ harmonic series.
     в—ү Direct Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ To prove convergence:
               в–  compare with a bigger convergent series.
          в—Ӣ Symbolically:
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $0\le рқ’Ӯ_{n}\le рқ’ғ_{n}$
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $\displaystyle \sum рқ’ғ_{n}$ converges
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  therefore, $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ converges.
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ To prove divergence:
               в–  compare with a smaller divergent series.
          в—Ӣ Symbolically:
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $0\le рқ’ғ_{n}\le рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $\displaystyle \sum рқ’ғ_{n}$ diverges
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  therefore, $\displaystyle \sum рқ’Ӯ_{n}$ diverges.
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Limit Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ Use when two positive-term series have similar end behavior.
          в—Ӣ Compute:
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
               в–  $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}{рқ’ғ_{n}}$
               $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ iМІfМІ the limit exists and is a finite positive number: $0<рқ‘і<\infty$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ both series have the same convergence behavior.
                    в–Ј they both converge or both diverge.

в—Ҹ [1:34:21](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=5661). вқә вҖ” Alternating Series Test.
     в—ү Use when the series has the form:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum (-1)^{n}рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ or $\displaystyle \sum (-1)^{n-1}рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Where:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}>0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Check two conditions:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ӯ_{n}=0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}\le рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү iМІfМІ both conditions hold:
          в—Ӣ tМІhМІeМІnМІ the alternating series converges.
     в—ү Important:
          в—Ӣ this proves ordinary convergence,
          в—Ӣ not necessarily absolute convergence.

в—Ҹ [1:36:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=5763). вқ» вҖ” Ratio Test.
     в—ү Try the Ratio Test for:
          в—Ӣ factorials.
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ$-th powers.
     в—ү Compute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left|\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}}{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}\right|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү iМІfМІ
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і<1$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ absolutely convergent.
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і>1$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ divergent.
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=1$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ inconclusive.

в—Ҹ [1:37:09](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=5829). вқј вҖ” Root Test.
     в—ү Try the Root Test for:
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ$-th powers.
     в—ү Compute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{|рқ’Ӯ_{n}|}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү iМІfМІ
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і<1$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ absolutely convergent.
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і>1$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ divergent.
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=1$
               в–  tМІhМІeМІnМІ inconclusive.

в—Ҹ [рҹ“·image-1](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.6/[1-40-19]-01.png) [рҹ“·image-2](../img/Calculus 2 Lecture 9.6/[1-40-19]-02.png)

в—Ҹ [1:40:19](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6019). вқ¶ Always start by looking for immediate divergence:
     в—ү if $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ӯ_{n}\ne 0$,
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ stop.
          в—Ӣ the series diverges.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{2рқ’Ҹ-1}{3рқ’Ҹ-1}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Check the general term:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{2рқ’Ҹ-1}{3рқ’Ҹ-1}=\dfrac{2}{3}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{2}{3}\ne 0$

     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the series diverges by the Divergence Test.


в—Ҹ [1:40:57](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6057). вқ· Split sums when possible.
     в—ү If a series is written as a sum or difference of recognizable series,
          в—Ӣ split it.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left[\dfrac{2}{3^{n}}-\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ(рқ’Ҹ+1)}\right]$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Split:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{2}{3^{n}}-\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ(рқ’Ҹ+1)}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү First part:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{2}{3^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}2\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ geometric series with $|рқ’“|=\dfrac{1}{3}<1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ therefore, it converges.
     в—ү Second part:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ(рқ’Ҹ+1)}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ telescoping series.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ analyze each part separately.
     в—ү The whole series converges only if all parts converge.

в—Ҹ [1:41:36](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6096). вқё Look for рқ’‘-Series.
     в—ү If the expression can be rewritten as:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{рқ’‘}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ use the рқ’‘-Series Test.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{рқ’Ҷ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Rewrite:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\left(\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ}\right)^{рқ’Ҷ}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{рқ’Ҷ}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү This is a рқ’‘-series with:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’‘=рқ’Ҷ$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҷ>1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{рқ’Ҷ}}$ converges.


в—Ҹ [1:42:05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6125). вқ№ Integral Test versus Comparison.
     в—ү Use the Integral Test when:
          в—Ӣ the function is easy to integrate,
          в—Ӣ and positive, continuous, decreasing.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=3}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ\sqrt{\ln(рқ’Ҹ)}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Model with:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)=\dfrac{1}{рқ’ҷ\sqrt{\ln(рқ’ҷ)}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү This suggests the Integral Test because:
          в—Ӣ the function is positive for $рқ’ҷ\ge 3$.
          в—Ӣ it is continuous for $рқ’ҷ\ge 3$.
          в—Ӣ it is decreasing for large $рқ’ҷ$.
          в—Ӣ the integral is manageable by substitution.
     в—ү Integral idea:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ let $рқ’–=\ln(рқ’ҷ)$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ then $рқ’…рқ’–=\dfrac{1}{рқ’ҷ}\cdot рқ’…рқ‘Ҙ$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ use the Integral Test.

в—Ҹ [1:42:38](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6158). вқә Direct Comparison for single dominant terms.
     в—ү If the expression has simple single-term behavior,
          в—Ӣ Direct Comparison may be easiest.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=3}^{\infty}\dfrac{\ln(рқ’Ҹ)}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Single-term comparison idea:
          в—Ӣ $\ln(рқ’Ҹ)$ grows slower than $\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}$.
     в—ү So for large $рқ’Ҹ$:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\ln(рқ’Ҹ)<\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{\ln(рқ’Ҹ)}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}<\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Simplify:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=3}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}$ is a рқ’‘-series with $рқ’‘=\dfrac{3}{2}>1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=3}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}$ converges.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү By Direct Comparison:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=3}^{\infty}\dfrac{\ln(рқ’Ҹ)}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}$ converges.


в—Ҹ [1:43:23](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6203). вқ» Limit Comparison for multiple-term expressions.
     в—ү If the numerator or denominator has several terms,
          в—Ӣ Direct Comparison may be awkward.
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ use leading terms.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}-2}}{рқ’Ҹ^{4}+3рқ’Ҹ^{2}+1}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү End behavior:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}-2}$ behaves like $\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}}=рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ^{4}+3рқ’Ҹ^{2}+1$ behaves like $рқ’Ҹ^{4}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}-2}}{рқ’Ҹ^{4}+3рқ’Ҹ^{2}+1}$ behaves like $\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}{рқ’Ҹ^{4}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Simplify:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}{рқ’Ҹ^{4}}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{5/2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Choose:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’ғ_{n}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{5/2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then compute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}{рқ’ғ_{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{5/2}}$ is a рқ’‘-series with $рқ’‘=\dfrac{5}{2}>1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ use the Limit Comparison Test.
          в—Ӣ the given series converges if the comparison limit is finite and positive.


в—Ҹ [1:44:15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6255). вқј Alternating series.
     в—ү If the series clearly has:
          в—Ӣ $(-1)^{n}$
          в—Ӣ or $(-1)^{n-1}$
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ try the Alternating Series Test.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(-1)^{n}\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}+1}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Positive part:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}=\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}+1}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Check:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ӯ_{n}=0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Reason:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}+1}$ behaves like $\dfrac{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ}}{рқ’Ҹ^{2}}=\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ and $\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}\to 0$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then check decreasing:
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}\le рқ’Ӯ_{n}$
          в—Ӣ or use $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)<0$ for the related function.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ try the Alternating Series Test.

в—Ҹ [1:44:33](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6273). вқҪ Ratio Test or Root Test.
     в—ү Use these when:
          в—Ӣ other tests are not convenient,
          в—Ӣ the expression has factorials,
          в—Ӣ or $рқ’Ҹ$-th powers.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү This is not directly geometric:
          в—Ӣ because of the extra factor $рқ’Ҹ$ in the numerator.
     в—ү (i) Use the Ratio Test:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n}=\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}=\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{2^{n+1}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Compute:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\left|\dfrac{рқ’Ӯ_{n+1}}{рқ’Ӯ_{n}}\right|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{2^{n+1}}\cdot \dfrac{2^{n}}{рқ’Ҹ}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ+1}{рқ’Ҹ}\cdot \dfrac{1}{2}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\dfrac{1}{2}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\dfrac{1}{2}<1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}$ converges by the Ratio Test.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү (ii) Root Test also works.
     в—ү Given:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Rewrite the numerator:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ=рқ’Ҹ^{1}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ^{1}=рқ’Ҹ^{n/n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ^{n/n}=(рқ’Ҹ^{1/n})^{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}=\dfrac{(рқ’Ҹ^{1/n})^{n}}{2^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{1/n}}{2}\right)^{n}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Apply the Root Test:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{\left|\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}\right|}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{\left(\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{1/n}}{2}\right)^{n}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ^{1/n}}{2}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}рқ’Ҹ^{1/n}=1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\dfrac{1}{2}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $рқ‘і=\dfrac{1}{2}<1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Therefore:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{рқ’Ҹ}{2^{n}}$ converges by the Root Test.


в—Ҹ [1:45:12](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g4iZJOwMkjU&t=6312). вқҫ Strategy for trigonometric functions.
     в—ү If the series contains:
          в—Ӣ $\sin(рқ’Ҹ)$,
          в—Ӣ $\cos(рқ’Ҹ)$,
          в—Ӣ $\sin(2рқ’Ҹ)$,
          в—Ӣ or similar oscillating terms,
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ it may not be positive,
          в—Ӣ and may not alternate regularly.
     в—ү вӯҗ Model Example:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{\sin(рқ’Ҹ)}{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}+1}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү In that case:
          в—Ӣ try absolute value.
     в—ү Take:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\left|\dfrac{\sin(рқ’Ҹ)}{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}+1}}\right|$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $=\dfrac{|\sin(рқ’Ҹ)|}{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}+1}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $|\sin(рқ’Ҹ)|\le 1$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{|\sin(рқ’Ҹ)|}{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}+1}}\le \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}+1}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү End behavior:
     $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{рқ’Ҹ^{3}+1}}$ behaves like $\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}$
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Since:
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{3/2}}$ converges
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
     в—ү Then:
          в—Ӣ use absolute value plus comparison.
     в—ү Therefore:
          в—Ӣ the original series is absolutely convergent.

в—Ҹ NМІOМІTМІEМІ вҖ” Final strategy.
     в—ү Do not randomly choose a test.
     в—ү First identify the structure:
          в—Ӣ nonzero term limit вҮ’ Divergence Test.
          в—Ӣ geometric form вҮ’ Geometric Series Test.
          в—Ӣ telescoping cancellation вҮ’ Telescoping Series.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ $\dfrac{1}{рқ’Ҹ^{рқ’‘}}$ form вҮ’ рқ’‘-Series.
          $\rule{0pt}{}$
          в—Ӣ positive continuous decreasing function вҮ’ Integral Test.
          в—Ӣ positive terms like a known series вҮ’ Comparison.
          в—Ӣ alternating signs вҮ’ Alternating Series Test.
          в—Ӣ factorials вҮ’ Ratio Test.
          в—Ӣ $рқ’Ҹ$-th powers вҮ’ Root Test.
          в—Ӣ trig oscillation вҮ’ absolute value plus comparison.
